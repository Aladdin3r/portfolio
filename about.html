About Me

I’m Rawan Aladdin (yes, like the movie), a vibrant designer who’s an avid history fanatic, classic movie-lover, amateur photographer and a big animal lover.

From the bustling corridors of the University of Toronto to the innovative labs at the British Columbia Institute of Technology, I’ve journeyed through the landscapes of Cognitive Science, Philosophy, Digital Humanities, and UX/UI Design. Armed with Figma, HTML, CSS, and a knack for Human-Computer Interaction, I craft experiences that delight and inspire.

When I'm not tinkering with prototypes or fine-tuning pixels, you'll find me diving into the latest trends in Artificial Intelligence, Augmented Reality, and UX methodologies. My adventures have taken me from redesigning web layouts for cultural associations to leading digital media teams, always with a camera in hand and a vision in mind.

Let’s create something amazing together! Explore my work, get inspired, and join me on this exciting journey of discovery and innovation.
