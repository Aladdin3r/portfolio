SACABC Website Redesign

As part of my internship, I led the redesign of the SACABC website, focusing on improving user experience and visual consistency. The project involved addressing issues such as information overload, inconsistent design elements, and poor navigation.

Before & After: About Page

Key Changes Implemented

  • Consistent Design: Unified the design elements such as icons, CTA texts, and section titles across the website.
  • Improved Navigation: Simplified the header by reducing clutter and adding dropdown menus for better user flow.
  • Content Hierarchy: Adjusted the size and weight of titles to create a clear visual hierarchy.
  • Accessibility: Improved readability by ensuring text contrasts well with the background and removing false affordances.
  • Reduced White Space: Balanced the white space to make the content more engaging and less overwhelming.

Footer Redesign

The original footer suffered from information overload and lacked hierarchy. I streamlined the footer by:

  • Reducing the number of links and organizing them into clear categories.
  • Removing redundant content and adding a link to the news archive.
  • Ensuring social media links were up-to-date and relevant.
